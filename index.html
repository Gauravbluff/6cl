Foreign Medical Scholarship Programme or FMSP is conducted and awarded by National Vidya Foundation for the students seeking MBBS Admission in Abroad. The university has no role in awarding scholarship. The college listed below are those in which this offer can be availed. Colleges are:

  • AMA School of Medicine, Philippines
